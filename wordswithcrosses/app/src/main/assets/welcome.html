Robo Crosswords

Thanks for downloading the Robo Crosswords crossword puzzle application! The application doesn't include any crossword puzzles out of the box, but it has now begun downloading puzzles from the last 7 days for you to solve. They will appear in the puzzle list once the downloads are complete, and you will get new puzzles for the day each time you start the app.

If you don't have a network connection right now, the downloads may fail. If this is the case, you can try again later by selecting the "Download" option available by pressing the Menu button on the selection screen.

Robo Crosswords is a hobby project for me, but feel free to email me at <robocrosswords@adamrosenfield.com> with your problems/suggestions. If you have a problem with a crash bug, please tap the "Send Debug Package" link in the Settings to send a log file to me to help diagnose your problem. If you're running a pre-4.1 version of Android OS, you can also download the SendLog app from Google Play and use it to send me the debug information.

Many thanks to Robert Cooper <kebernet@gmail.com> for creating the open-source Shortyz Crosswords app, from which this app was forked.

Keep circulating the tapes.
